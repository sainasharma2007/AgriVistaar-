# Product Overview

**AgriVistaar – Kisan ka Sathi** is a precision agriculture web application designed for Indian farmers. The platform helps farmers make data-driven decisions about crop management and market timing.

## Core Features

- **Drone Image Analysis**: Upload and analyze drone-captured field images to detect pest attacks, nutrient deficiencies, and yield variations
- **Field Health Monitoring**: Track multiple fields with real-time health status, pest risk levels, and nutrient stress indicators
- **Yield Prediction**: AI/ML pipeline estimates crop yield per acre to help farmers plan harvest
- **Mandi Price Intelligence**: Combines crop health data with market trends to suggest optimal selling times and locations
- **Profit Calculator**: Estimate revenue and profit based on yield predictions and market prices
- **Scan Planning**: Smart recommendations for when to conduct drone scans at critical growth stages
- **Multi-language Support**: Available in English, Hindi, Tamil, Bengali, Marathi, and Telugu

## Target Users

Indian farmers and FPOs (Farmer Producer Organizations) who want to leverage drone technology and AI for better crop management and market decisions.

## User Flow

1. Farmers sign up with phone/email
2. Add their fields with crop details
3. Request drone scans at key growth stages
4. View AI-analyzed health reports (pest risk, nutrient stress, yield estimates)
5. Check mandi price signals
6. Calculate expected profit
7. Get AI assistant support for farming questions
